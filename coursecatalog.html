Understanding Aligned and Non-Aligned Courses

At Southwest Technical College, our program catalog includes courses designed to meet both state and regional needs. The information below explains the difference between aligned and non-aligned courses.

Aligned Courses
Aligned courses follow a standardized framework shared across all state institutions. These courses have the same course ID, name, description, and objectives, ensuring consistency and transferability within the state. Aligned courses are designed to provide a uniform foundation of knowledge and skills that meet statewide industry standards.

Non-Aligned Courses
Non-aligned courses are tailored to meet the specific needs of regional employers and industry partners. These courses focus on skills and training that are directly relevant to the local job market, helping students become competitive and job-ready within our region.
